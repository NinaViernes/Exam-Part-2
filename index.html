In Her Element

Nina is an asian artist who loves to create beautiful pottery. Her works are inspired by the nature and culture of the Philippines. Her handmade pieces reflect the beauty of the land, the sea, and the traditions that have been passed down through generations. Each of her piece brings a sense of history, culture, and life. She also tries to capture the warmth and heart of her beautiful country. Her work also includes special patterns and vibrant colors that will remind you of landscapes and the depths of the blue ocean. All of Nina's pieces tells a story and a connection to her people and homeland.
