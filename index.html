About Nina

Nina is a Filipino artist who creates beautiful pottery inspired by the nature and culture of the Philippines. Her handmade pieces reflect the beauty of the land, the sea, and the traditions passed down through generations. Each piece is made with care and connects to the spirit of the Philippines, bringing a sense of its history and culture to life.

Using both traditional and modern techniques, Nina’s pottery is more than just decoration; it’s a way to feel the warmth and spirit of the Philippines in your home.
